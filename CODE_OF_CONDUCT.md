# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:

- Being respectful and constructive
- Accepting feedback gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the community and product

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks
- Public or private abuse
- Publishing private information without explicit permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing standards.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues to:

- `opensource@anakwe.org`

All reports will be reviewed and handled confidentially.
